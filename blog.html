How to Reduce Your Carbon Footprint

Here are some practical tips for reducing your carbon footprint:

  • Use public transportation, carpool, or bike instead of driving alone
  • Switch to energy-efficient appliances and light bulbs
  • Eat less meat and dairy products
  • Buy locally grown and produced food

The Importance of Recycling

Recycling helps to conserve resources and reduce waste:

  • Recycling aluminum cans saves 95% of the energy needed to make new cans from raw materials
  • Recycling one ton of paper saves 17 trees and 7,000 gallons of water
  • Recycling plastic bottles saves enough energy to power a laptop for 25 hours
